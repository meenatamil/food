Ingredients for Tomato Juice

  • Main Ingredient:

    4-5 ripe, juicy tomatoes (medium-sized).

  • Optional Enhancers:

    A pinch of salt (to taste). A pinch of black pepper (for a slight kick).

  • Tanginess

    1–2 teaspoons of fresh lemon juice.

  • Herbs:

    Optional: a few fresh basil or mint leaves for aroma.

  • Consistency Adjuster:

    1/4 cup water or a few ice cubes (to thin and chill the juice).

    How to Make Tomato Juice

  1. Prepare the Tomatoes

    Wash 4–5 ripe tomatoes thoroughly and cut them into small pieces for easy blending

  2. Blend the Tomatoes

    Add the tomato pieces to a blender along with 1/4 cup water. Blend until smooth.

  3. Strain the Juice

    Use a fine sieve or cheesecloth to strain the juice, removing the seeds and skin for a smoother texture.

  4. Season the Juice

    Add a pinch of salt, black pepper, and 1–2 teaspoons of lemon juice for flavor. Mix well.

  5. Serve and Enjoy

    Pour the juice into a glass, add ice cubes if desired, and garnish with fresh mint or basil leaves. Serve chilled!
