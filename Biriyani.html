Main Ingredients

  • The main ingredients of biryani are basmati rice, protein (like chicken, mutton, or vegetables).
  • It also includes aromatics like ginger-garlic paste, onions, and herbs like mint and coriander.
  • Optional enhancers include saffron, yogurt, and ghee for richness and flavor.

How to Make Biryani in 5 Simple Steps

  1. Cook the Rice

    Wash and soak basmati rice, then cook it with whole spices (like bay leaves, cardamom, and cloves) until 70% done. Drain and set aside.

  2. Prepare the Protein

    Marinate chicken, mutton, or vegetables with yogurt, ginger-garlic paste, spices, and salt. Cook in a pan until tender and flavorful.

  3. Layer the Biryani

    In a pot, layer cooked protein at the bottom, then add a layer of semi-cooked rice. Sprinkle fried onions, mint, coriander, and saffron milk on top.

    Gently mix the layers before serving hot with raita or salad.
